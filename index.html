Have you ever heard of the Spring Festival? The Spring Festival is the most important traditional festival in China, symbolizing "reunion" "gratitude" and "hope". For Chinese people, instead of New Year’s day, the Spring Festival marks the beginning of a New Year. The year 2021 is the Year of the OX on Chinese lunar calendar. As a symbol of diligence and honesty, the OX also has auspicious connotations. In Chinese language, there are many OX-related idioms, such as Niu Qi Cong Tian , Niu Yun Heng Tong and Niu Jing Shi Zu , which describe the spirit of entrepreneurship, the everlasting fortune and the great power respectively. On the arrival of the Chinese Lunar New Year in 2021, hand in hand, Sichuan Xinhua Publishing and Distribution Group and Burggymnasium Essen bring an online Spring Festival banquet for overseas Chinese people and friends all over the world. The ruthless epidemic cannot prevent us from enhancing the friendship with each other, as we can still get together in the "cloud" to share the joy, peace and warmth of the New Year.
