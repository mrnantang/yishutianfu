The observations of “national treasure”


-1-The scene of fighting   Come to mediate
A panda followed another one. In the end, the two pandas huddled together and used "tricks" such as biting the tails and scratching the heads.

-2- Save the baby
On a crisp fall day, a panda climbed up a tree to have fun. However, he climbed too high and didn't know how to get down.

-3- Are you awake, my boy?
The pandas are so cute when they just wake up. Some bounce like a spring, some slowly get up lazily; some sit in a daze; some just wake up and turn to eat. Did you get the adorable performance of pandas today?

-4- The daily kidding of baby pandas
Two mischievous pandas were fighting, and then another one came to “mediate” and ended the "battle", which added a pleasure to this boring afternoon.

-5- Easily subduing the “naughty baby panda” with a trick
A naughty baby panda was playing with tree branches, and then subdued by the mother with a trick.

-6-The expression of drinking milk is the happiest performance to all those present.
It was really the happiest time for baby pandas whenever the breeder "mom" came to the sun delivery room with a bottle full of milk to feed the babies.
