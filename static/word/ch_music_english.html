Introduction

The purposes of this activity aim at fully demonstrating the charm of Chinese and the connotation of Chinese culture through traditional Chinese festival cultures and the special “panda” cultures of Bashu. And this online exhibition will also contains the details of UAE’s local festival cultures and customs in celebrations in order to improve the cultural exchanges and interaction between China and the United Arab Emirates. To improve the knowledge of Chinese cultures understood by Chinese language and culture learners and addicts in UAE and around the world, and promoting the cultural exchanges and mutual learning between Chinese and foreign languages and cultures.
