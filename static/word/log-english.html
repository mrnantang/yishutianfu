The observations of “national treasure”

-1- the scene of fighting, and waiting for persuading
A panda followed another one. In the end, the two pandas huddled together and used "tricks" such as biting the tails and scratching the heads.
-2- Please save the kid
On a presently cool day in autumn, a panda climbed up a tree to play. As a result, he climbed too high and didn't know how to get down.
-3- Are you awake, my boy?
The pandas are so cute when they just wake up. Some spring up like a mechanical spring, some slowly get up lazily; some sit in a daze; some just wake up and turn to eat. Did you get the adorable performance of pandas’ today?
-4- The daily kidding of baby pandas’
Two mischievous pandas were fighting, and then came another one to “persuade the fight” and ended the "battle", which added a pleasure to this boring afternoon.
-5- Easily subdued the “naughty baby panda”
A naughty baby panda was playing with branches, and then subdued by the mother.
-6- The expression of drinking milk is the happiest performance of all those present.
It was really the happiest time for baby pandas whenever the breeder \"mom\" came to the sun delivery room with a bottle full of milk to feed the babies.
