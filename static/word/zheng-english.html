Easy Steps to Zither

Zither, known as the “Oriental Piano”, is a treasure of Chinese national musical instruments, as well as one of the most representative musical instruments of China.
“The Moon over the River on a Spring Night”, an ancient Chinese music song played through zither in the Opening Ceremony of the 2008 Beijing Olympic Games, let the whole world indulge in the wonderful zither music.
Do you prefer zither? Do you want to learn it? The “Easy Steps to Zither” can help you learn the rudiments of Zither quickly and easily!
